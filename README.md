## 🧬 About Me

I sit at the intersection of **pharmaceutical science** and **AI engineering** — building intelligent systems that make pharma knowledge accessible, automated, and actionable.

- 🔭 Currently building **PharmaForge AI** — an agentic AI execution platform for pharma content
- 🌍 Running **[PharmaLeap.in](https://pharmaleap.in)** — a pharma knowledge hub for India
- 🤖 Working with **Gemini 1.5 Flash**, **NVIDIA LLaMA 3.1 70B**, and custom **RAG pipelines**
- 🧪 Domain: Drug Regulatory Affairs, Pharma R&D, AI-driven content generation
- 💡 Believer in open-source pharma infrastructure
